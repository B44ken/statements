# Vancouver (British Columbia) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder, `FinancialReport.pdf` is the City of Vancouver "Annual Financial Report" (the City's published annual financial report PDF).

## Coverage

This folder contains annual financial reports from 2002 to 2024 (23 years total).

## Source

City of Vancouver – Financial Reports and Information:
https://vancouver.ca/your-government/financial-reports-and-information.aspx

All reports were downloaded from this page on December 16, 2025.

## Notes

- Vancouver has comprehensive financial reporting going back to 2002
- The reports are consistently titled "Annual Financial Report"
- File sizes range from ~500 KB to ~7 MB
- All downloads were successful with no blockers encountered
