# Delta (British Columbia) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2016-2024), `AnnualReport.pdf` is the City of Delta "Annual Report" which includes the audited consolidated financial statements along with narrative content about the city's activities and performance.

## Years covered

- **2024**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2024)
- **2023**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2023)
- **2022**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2022)
- **2021**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2021)
- **2020**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2020)
- **2019**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2019)
- **2018**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2018)
- **2017**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2017)
- **2016**: Annual Report (Year Ended December 31, 2016)

## Source

City of Delta – Financial Information:
https://www.delta.ca/city-hall/municipal-information/financial-information

The annual reports are accessed through media links on this page. As required under the Community Charter, Delta prepares these Annual Reports to communicate to residents the activities and financial statements from the preceding year.

## Notes

- All available annual reports from 2016-2024 have been downloaded (9 years total)
- Each annual report includes comprehensive financial statements along with narrative content
- The 2016 report is significantly larger (23MB) compared to other years, possibly due to different formatting or additional content
