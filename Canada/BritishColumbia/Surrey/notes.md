# Surrey (British Columbia) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2018-2024), `AnnualFinancialReport.pdf` is the City of Surrey "Annual Financial Report" which includes the city's audited financial statements, statistics, activities, accomplishments and performance measures.

## Notes

- Surrey's Annual Financial Reports are prepared pursuant to s.98 of the Community Charter.
- The reports include audited financial statements and are presented at public meetings before June 29 of each year.
- The city also publishes Statement of Financial Information (SOFI) documents separately, which include additional details on remuneration and supplier payments.
- Interactive/flipbook versions of recent reports are also available on the city's website but PDFs were downloaded for archival purposes.
- Earlier years (pre-2018) were not found on the current website - they may be archived elsewhere or not publicly available online.
