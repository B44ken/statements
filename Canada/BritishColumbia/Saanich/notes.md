# Saanich (British Columbia) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2010-2024), `AnnualReport.pdf` is the District of Saanich's Annual Report, which includes the Consolidated Financial Statements.

## Coverage

The Annual Reports contain:
- Consolidated Financial Statements (audited)
- Management's Discussion and Analysis
- Strategic Plan progress updates
- Performance measures and statistics

## Source

District of Saanich – Corporate & Annual Reports:
https://www.saanich.ca/EN/main/local-government/corporate-annual-reports/annual-reports.html

All reports (2010-2024) were downloaded from the above page on December 16, 2024.

## Notes

- The Annual Reports include the full audited Consolidated Financial Statements prepared in accordance with Canadian public sector accounting standards
- Reports are comprehensive and include both financial statements and performance reporting
- All 15 years of available reports (2010-2024) have been successfully downloaded
