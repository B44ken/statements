# Abbotsford (British Columbia) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2010-2024), `FinancialStatements.pdf` contains the City of Abbotsford's Consolidated Financial Statements.

## Coverage

- **2010-2024**: 15 years of annual consolidated financial statements available

## Source

City of Abbotsford – Finance:
https://www.abbotsford.ca/city-hall/finance

All financial statements were obtained from the "Financial Statements" section of the finance page, which provides consolidated financial statements for each year.

## Notes

- The city provides comprehensive financial statements dating back to 2010
- All statements are consolidated financial statements
- Some years are labeled as part of the "Annual Report" (e.g., 2020, 2022, 2023)
