# Burnaby (British Columbia) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2020-2024), `AnnualMunicipalReport.pdf` contains the City of Burnaby's Annual Municipal Report, which includes the audited consolidated financial statements along with other municipal information.

## Years Available

- **2024**: Annual Municipal Report
- **2023**: Annual Municipal Report  
- **2022**: Annual Municipal Report
- **2021**: Annual Municipal Report
- **2020**: Annual Municipal Report

## Source

City of Burnaby – Financial & Annual Reports:
https://www.burnaby.ca/our-city/financial-and-annual-reports

## Notes

- The Annual Municipal Reports include audited consolidated financial statements.
- The City also publishes Financial Plan documents and Statement of Financial Information (SOFI), which are available on the same page but not included here as the Annual Municipal Reports contain the audited financial statements.
- Reports from 2020-2024 (5 years) are currently available on the City's website.
