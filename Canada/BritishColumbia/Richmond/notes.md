# Richmond (British Columbia) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder, `AnnualReport.pdf` is the City of Richmond "Annual Report" (the City's published annual financial report PDF). These comprehensive reports include financial statements, operations overviews, and major achievement highlights.

## Coverage

- **2020-2024**: Complete annual reports (comprehensive versions)

The City of Richmond publishes both a comprehensive annual report and a highlights version. This folder contains the comprehensive versions which include full financial statements.

## Source

City of Richmond – Annual Reports:
https://www.richmond.ca/city-hall/finance/reporting/reports.htm

## Notes

The webpage shows annual reports from 2020 onwards. Earlier years may exist but were not readily available on the current Annual Reports page.
