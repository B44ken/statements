# Caledon (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2015-2024), `FinancialReport.pdf` contains the Town of Caledon's consolidated financial statements or annual financial reports.

## Available Years

The following financial statements have been downloaded and organized:

- **2024**: Town of Caledon 2024 Financial Statements (545 KB)
- **2023**: Town of Caledon 2023 Financial Statements (469 KB)
- **2022**: Financial Report and Consolidated Financial Statements (16 MB)
- **2021**: Consolidated Annual Financial Report (11 MB)
- **2020**: Consolidated Annual Financial Report (2.6 MB)
- **2019**: Town of Caledon Financial Statements (11 MB)
- **2018**: Financial Statements (12 MB)
- **2017**: Financial Statements (7.2 MB)
- **2016**: Financial Statements (6.6 MB)
- **2015**: Financial Statements (6.3 MB)

## Source

Town of Caledon – Budget and Finances:
https://www.caledon.ca/en/government/budget-and-finances.aspx

All financial statements were retrieved from the "Financial statements" and "Past Financial Statements" sections of the Budget and Finances page.

## Notes

- The Town's website lists financial statements from 2015 onwards
- More recent years (2023-2024) appear to be consolidated financial statements only, while earlier years may include full annual reports
- The 2022 document is notably larger (16 MB) and may include additional reporting beyond just financial statements
- No blockers were encountered during the download process
