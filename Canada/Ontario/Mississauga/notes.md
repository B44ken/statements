# Mississauga (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2019-2024), `FinancialReport.pdf` contains the City of Mississauga's Annual Financial and Sustainability Report (or Annual Financial Report for 2019).

These reports include:
- Consolidated financial statements
- Auditor's report
- Notes to the consolidated financial statements
- Statistical information
- Sustainability reporting (2020 onwards)

## Notes

- The city transitioned to "Financial and Sustainability Report" naming starting in 2020
- Prior to 2020, reports were called "Annual Financial Report"
- All reports are comprehensive and include audited consolidated financial statements
- Reports are typically published in September of the following year
