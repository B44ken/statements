# Essex (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

This folder contains audited financial statements for the Town of Essex, Ontario, from 2018 to 2024.

For each year folder, `FinancialStatements.pdf` is the Corporation of the Town of Essex "Audited Financial Statements" (the Town's published annual consolidated financial statements PDF).

## Available Years

- **2024**: Audited Financial Statements for year ending December 31, 2024
- **2023**: Audited Financial Statements for year ending December 31, 2023
- **2022**: Audited Financial Statements for year ending December 31, 2022
- **2021**: Audited Financial Statements for year ending December 31, 2021
- **2020**: Audited Financial Statements for year ending December 31, 2020
- **2019**: Audited Financial Statements for year ending December 31, 2019
- **2018**: Audited Financial Statements for year ending December 31, 2018

## Source

Town of Essex – Budgets and Financial Reports:
https://www.essex.ca/en/town-hall/budgetsandfinancialreports.aspx

## Additional Information

The Town of Essex also publishes:
- Capital and Operating Budgets (available from 2019-2025)
- Four-Year Budget Forecasts (available from 2020 onwards)

These budget documents are available at the same source URL above but were not included in this collection as the focus was on audited financial statements.
