# Hamilton (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2014-2022), `FinancialReport.pdf` contains the City of Hamilton's consolidated financial report.

## Years Available

Financial reports are available for the following years:
- 2014 through 2022

## Blockers

- 2023 and 2024 financial reports are not yet available on the City of Hamilton website as of December 2025.
- The website only provides reports up to 2022.

## Source

City of Hamilton – Financial Results:
https://www.hamilton.ca/city-council/city-administration/financial-results

All financial reports were downloaded from this page.
