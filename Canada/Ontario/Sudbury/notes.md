# Sudbury (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year from 2019 to 2024:
- `AnnualReport.pdf` - City of Greater Sudbury Annual Report (comprehensive annual financial report)
- `ConsolidatedFinancialStatements.pdf` - City of Greater Sudbury Consolidated Financial Statements (audited financial statements)

## Years covered

- 2024
- 2023
- 2022
- 2021
- 2020
- 2019

## Source

City of Greater Sudbury – Financial Reports:
https://www.greatersudbury.ca/city-hall/budget-and-finance/financial-reports/

## Notes

- The City of Greater Sudbury (official name) is commonly referred to as just "Sudbury"
- Financial statements and annual reports are available on the city's website going back to 2013 (and some earlier years)
- This folder contains the most recent 6 years (2019-2024) of both annual reports and consolidated financial statements
- Older years (2013-2018) are also available on the website but were not downloaded to focus on recent years
- All PDFs were successfully downloaded with no access issues (firewall previously disabled)

## Additional documents available (not downloaded)

The city also publishes for each year:
- Financial Information Return (FIR)
- Trust Funds statements
- Sinking Fund statements
- Financial statements for related entities (e.g., Greater Sudbury Housing Corporation, Greater Sudbury Utilities Inc., etc.)
- Presentation to Audit Committee

These documents are available on the source website if needed in the future.
