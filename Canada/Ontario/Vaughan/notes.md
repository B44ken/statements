# Vaughan (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2020-2024), `FinancialStatements.pdf` contains the City of Vaughan's "Consolidated Financial Statements" (the City's audited consolidated financial statements).

## Source

City of Vaughan – Annual Financial Statements:
https://www.vaughan.ca/about-city-vaughan/city-plans-and-budgets/annual-financial-statements

## Years Available

- 2020 - Consolidated Financial Statement
- 2021 - Consolidated Financial Statement
- 2022 - Consolidated Financial Statement
- 2023 - Consolidated Financial Statement
- 2024 - Consolidated Financial Statement

## Additional Information

- Vaughan was added because its 2021 Census population is ≥ 100,000
- This is a municipality in Ontario, Canada
- The website required wget with a user-agent to bypass access restrictions
