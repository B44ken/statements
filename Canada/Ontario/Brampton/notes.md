# Brampton (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

Annual Reports for the City of Brampton from 2003 to 2024.

Each year folder contains an `AnnualReport.pdf` file which is the City's published annual financial report.

## Years Available

- 2024 to 2003 (22 years total)
- All annual reports include consolidated financial statements

## Source

City of Brampton – Annual Reports:
https://www.brampton.ca/EN/City-Hall/Annual-Report/Pages/AnnualReport.aspx

## Notes

- All annual reports were successfully downloaded
- The City's website also provides Financial Information Return (FIR) documents for most years, but only the Annual Reports were downloaded as they contain the comprehensive financial statements
- No blockers encountered during download
