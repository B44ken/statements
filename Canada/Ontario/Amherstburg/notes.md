# Amherstburg (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder, `FinancialStatements.pdf` contains the Town of Amherstburg's Audited Financial Statements for that year.

## Years Available

The Town of Amherstburg website currently provides audited financial statements for:
- 2023 (most recent)
- 2022

## Source

Town of Amherstburg – Financial Statements:
https://www.amherstburg.ca/town-hall/budget-and-finance/financial-statements/

### Direct Links

- 2023 Financial Statements: https://www.amherstburg.ca/media/sxjlr5q2/2023-12-31-town-of-amherstburg-fs-rm-signed-1.pdf
- 2022 Financial Statements: https://www.amherstburg.ca/media/rrbl504k/2022-12-31-town-of-amherstburg-fs-rm.pdf

## Additional Resources

The Town also publishes Annual Treasurer's Reports (remuneration and expenses) which are available at:
https://www.amherstburg.ca/town-hall/budget-and-finance/financial-reports/

These reports are available for years 2012-2024 but are different from the audited financial statements.

## Blockers/Notes

- Only 2022 and 2023 audited financial statements are available on the Town's website
- Older financial statements (pre-2022) do not appear to be publicly available online
- The municipality may have older statements available upon request to their Finance department
