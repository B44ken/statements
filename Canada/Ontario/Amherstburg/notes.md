## How to Complete This Task

1. **Visit the Town of Amherstburg website:**
   - Main site: https://www.amherstburg.ca
   
2. **Navigate to financial reports section:**
   - Try: https://www.amherstburg.ca/en/town-hall/financial-reports.aspx
   - Or search the site for "financial statements", "annual reports", or "budget"
   
3. **Download financial statements:**
   - Look for "Consolidated Financial Statements" or "Annual Financial Report"
   - Download PDFs for years 2022, 2023, 2024 (and any other available years)
   
4. **Add files to this repository:**
   - Place 2022 statements in the `2022/` folder
   - Place 2023 statements in the `2023/` folder
   - Place 2024 statements in the `2024/` folder
   - Name files consistently (e.g., `FinancialReport.pdf` or `ConsolidatedFinancialStatements.pdf`)
   - Remove the README.md files from year folders after adding actual PDFs
   
5. **Update this notes.md:**
   - Add the actual source URL
   - Document what files were added
   - Note any missing years or gaps in coverage
