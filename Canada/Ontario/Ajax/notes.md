# Ajax (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder, `FinancialStatements.pdf` contains the Town of Ajax's audited consolidated financial statements.

## Available Years

- **2024**: The Corporation of the Town of Ajax - 2024-12-31 - Financial Statements (AODA)
- **2023**: Town of Ajax - 2023 Audited Financial Statements
- **2022**: 2022 Financial Statements

## Notes

The Town of Ajax's annual financial reports page provides audited financial statements for recent years. As of the time of collection (December 2024), statements for 2022, 2023, and 2024 were available.

Older years (2021 and earlier) were not found on the current website.
