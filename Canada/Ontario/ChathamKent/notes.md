# ChathamKent (Ontario) – Notes

## What's in this folder

For each year folder (2018-2024), `FinancialStatement.pdf` contains the Municipality of Chatham-Kent's audited financial statements.

All financial statements are audited by a chartered professional accounting firm and prepared in accordance with Canadian generally accepted accounting principles for local governments as recommended by the Public Sector Accounting Board of the Canadian Institute of Chartered Accountants.

- 2023: Financial Statement  
- 2022: Financial Statement
- 2021: Financial Statement

- 2019: Financial Statement
- 2018: Financial Statement

## Notes

Financial statements are made available on the municipality's website after they have been accepted by the Audit Committee and Council, which typically happens during the summer months following year-end.
