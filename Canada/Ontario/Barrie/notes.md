## Search Status

Searched extensively for Barrie's audited financial statements and annual financial reports.

## Findings

The City of Barrie website (https://www.barrie.ca/) has budget documents available but does NOT appear to have audited annual financial statements publicly posted online.

### What Was Found:
- **Budget Documents**: Operating budgets and capital plans are available at:
  - https://www.barrie.ca/government-news/budget/previous-budgets
  - Available years: 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025
  - These are BUDGET documents (future planning), not audited financial statements (past year results)

### What Was NOT Found:
- No audited consolidated financial statements
- No annual financial reports
- No PSAB-compliant financial statements
- The financial statements page appears to have been removed or doesn't exist
  - Attempted URL: https://www.barrie.ca/government-news/budget-finances/financial-statements
  - Result: Page not found (404)
